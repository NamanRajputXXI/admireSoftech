FAQ

Digital marketing is the promotion of products or services using digital technologies. It involves various online channels like social media, search engines, email, and content creation to connect with audiences, build brand awareness, drive traffic, and generate sales. It encompasses diverse strategies such as SEO, PPC advertising, social media marketing, and content marketing to reach and engage target consumers in the digital realm.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of enhancing a website's visibility in search engine results. It involves optimizing website content, structure, and links to increase organic (non-paid) traffic. Strategies include keyword research, content optimization, meta tags, and improving user experience. The goal is to rank higher on search engines, boosting visibility and attracting more relevant visitors.

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites. It involves various tasks, including web design, coding, programming, and configuring web applications. Developers use programming languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build the structure and functionality of websites. This process encompasses both front-end (client-side) and back-end (server-side) development to ensure a functional and visually appealing online presence.
